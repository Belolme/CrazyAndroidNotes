Broadcast receiver

系统级的监听接收器, 拥有独立的进程, 发出的Broadcast会按优先级顺序被接收. 如果被中止,则无法接收到.

register broadcast receiver

在XML中注册Broadcast Receiver 

<receiver>
    <intent-filter priority="-1000..1000"/>
    <!--优先级顺序从小到大-->
</receiver>

或者使用registerReceiver(IntentFilter)
